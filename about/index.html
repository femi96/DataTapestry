Data Tapestry

Data Tapestry is a project to explore the data of Earth Tapestry. Earth Tapestry identifies locations on our planet that embody important qualities such as great wonder and rich information. The project archives this geographical information in several ways, including on the near side of the Moon. Earth Tapestry is built by William Alba and his team at Carnegie Mellon, in collaboration with Travis Rich and Kevin Hu at the MIT Media Lab. Earth Tapestry is built on top of the Quantify framework.

Data Tapestry was built and designed by Femi Oladipupo (with bits of input from Travis Rich) at the MIT Media Lab.
